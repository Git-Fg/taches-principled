Create a handoff document at `whats-next.md` capturing everything needed to continue this work.

Include:
- **Original task**: What was requested (scope, not side tasks)
- **Completed**: Files created/modified, key changes, decisions made
- **Remaining**: Specific next steps with file paths and targets
- **Blockers**: What didn't work, errors encountered, dead ends
- **Context**: Critical knowledge, assumptions, configuration details
- **Current state**: What's saved, what's draft, where you stopped

Prioritize precision over brevity. The goal is zero information loss for the next session.
