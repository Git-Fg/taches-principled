Evaluate the skill at $ARGUMENTS.

## Goal
Determine if this skill will be effective when invoked.

## What Matters

**Clarity** — Is the goal obvious? Can Claude understand what to do?

**Conciseness** — Does every line earn its place? Is there bloat?

**Routability** — Will Claude know when to invoke this? Is the description specific?

**Examples** — Are they helpful? Do they show the pattern?

## Output

Provide findings with file:line references. Be specific. Don't just say "improve X" — say exactly what to change.
